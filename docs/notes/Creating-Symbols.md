## Removing Libraries

1. Open `Symbol Editor`
2. On Top Left, click `Preferences`
3. Click `Manage Symbol Libraries...`
4. Click the one you want to remove and click the Trashcan near the bottom

> **NOTE** Libraries that you import will probably all be at the very bottom
